# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
HarTan Labs Claude Skills Vault a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on ideas and content, not on people.
- Acknowledge mistakes, learn from them, and move on.
- Prioritize what is best for the community and the project.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull
Requests, Discussions, commit messages, and any other official communication
channel associated with this project.

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces
(e.g., mentioning the project in social media posts, conference talks, or
community events).

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by opening a GitHub Issue or contacting the maintainer directly via
GitHub.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

**Consequences for violations:**

| Severity | Behavior | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Correction** | Unprofessional or unwelcome language | Private written warning; public apology may be requested |
| **2 — Warning** | Single serious violation or pattern of minor ones | Temporary restriction from project interaction |
| **3 — Temporary Ban** | Sustained inappropriate behavior | Temporary ban from all project spaces |
| **4 — Permanent Ban** | Severe violation, harassment, or threats | Permanent removal from all project spaces |
